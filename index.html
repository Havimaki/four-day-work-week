Justin Trudeau Announces
Four-Day Work Week
Starting This Fall

Colin Leggett

4 hours ago

It appears that Justin Trudeau has made the final call for all Canadian workplaces. Starting as early as September 21, all companies will be migrating over to a four-day work week, excluding the elevator industry.

“With the opening of schools,” Trudeau spoke during his latest announcement, “We feel it is best to take preventative measures and minimize the flow of people in our communities.”

With that statement, he cautioned workers to use the newly acquired third day weekend as a means to stay indoors, with the upcoming cooler temperatures. “We hope this will help contain and prevent any relapse in numbers”

This decision will affect all industries across the board, including construction, transportation, healthcare workers, amongst others. This decision will not be affecting civil servants, nor the elevator industry.
